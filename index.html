My Core Values

In any interaction involving more than one person, communication is absolutely crucial. In my eyes, this is especially important in the tech industry. The ability to communicate ideas with teammates who have knowledge over the same subjects you do, as well as with customers and contractors who may not is one of, if not the most important trait a person can have.

To many people, employers especially, being a good leader is a very important trait to have. To me, leadership isn't quite the right word. I believe that, to be an effective leader, as well as a team member, you have to be confident in yourself, your work, and the knowledge that you have and are willing to offer to your teammates or the people working below you. If you aren't confident in the things you say, how can anyone else place their faith in you to get a job done right?

One of the most important things I have learned throughout my time as a student, as well as an employee is to have proper work ethic. For me, being paid for a job means that you should do everything in your power to do that job to the best of your ability. I believe this same concept applies to learning as well. If you can work to better yourself in an area that is meaningful to you, you should, without question, do so.
